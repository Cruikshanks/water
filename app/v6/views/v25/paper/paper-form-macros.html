2. Did you abstract water?

6. Meter details

If you have used more than one meter
tell us about the current one.

7. and 8. Start and end meter readings

The start reading is what your meter read before you began abstracting in this period.

The end reading is taken at the end of the abstraction period. We treat all readings in between the start and end dates as if they were taken at the end of each {{phase}}.

If you used more than one meter
you need to enter volumes and not
meter readings.

A decimal point should only be used
to indicate a fraction of a whole unit.
For example, 0.5 will be taken to mean
half a gallon.

9. Meter readings or abstraction volumes

In every numbered line on the form,
you should enter a meter reading or the
actual quantity on the dates you abstracted
water and ‘0’ when you did not.
