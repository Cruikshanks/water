Licence no. 123457

Source of supply Underground strata comprising of chalk at Bath Spring Well, Charlton, Hitchin, Hertfordshire.

Point of abstraction
At National Grid Reference TL 1794 2809 marked ‘Abstraction Point’ on the map.

Purpose of abstraction
Transfer for the purpose of supporting flows within the River Hiz as part of the River Hiz Support Scheme, discharging water at National Grid Reference TL 17883 28104 marked ‘Discharge Point’ on the map.

Means of abstraction
A well not exceeding 5.75 metres in depth with the following dimensions:

0 to 3 metres deep
3.2 metres diameter
Pump type: Submersible pump

3 to 4.5 metres deep
2.8 metres diameter
Pump type: Submersible pump

4.5 to 5.75 metres deep
1.25 metres diameter
Pump type: Submersible pump

Means of measurement
(i) No abstraction shall take place unless the Licence Holder has installed a meter (or other measuring device) in the delivery pipe to measure quantities of water abstracted.

(ii) The Licence Holder shall position and install the meter in accordance with any written directions given by the Agency.

(iii) The Licence Holder shall maintain, repair or replace the meter to ensure that accurate measurements are recorded at all times.

(iv) The Licence Holder shall keep all records of meter repair or replacement including evidence of current certification for a period of 6 years.

Records
(i) The Licence Holder shall take and record readings of the meter specified in condition 7 at the same time each month during the whole of the period during which abstraction is authorised or as otherwise approved in writing by the Agency.

(ii) The Licence Holder shall send a copy of the record or summary data from it to the Agency within 28 calendar days of 31 March in each year or within 28 days of being so directed in writing by the Agency.

(iii) Each record shall be kept and be made available during all reasonable hours for inspection by the Agency for at least 6 years.
